    • VPN

    VPN- Virtual Private Network Establishes a secure and reliable network connection over unsecure network such as the Internet. Protects your internet activity and disguises your identity on the Internet.

    ISP- Internet Service Provider.

    Internet Service Provider can see and log the activity of a user connected to their server. There is a possibility that ISP share user’s activity with government or advertisers. VPN bypasses ISP server and redirects connection to VPN server. ISP can’t see Internet activity of a user. A VPN will disguise someone’s identity by hiding their IP address. Websites will see only IP Address of VPN server.

      Use of VPN

    • A VPN condense the data with an extra layer of protection.
    • A VPN enable people to access restricted internet content across the world.
    • A VPN encrypts and protect internet data.
    • A VPN provides a secure connection for remote workers.

    For whom is VPN?

    VPN serves anyone who wants to be extra safe on the internet. It provides safety, security and anonymity. Whoever uses public WiFi should use VPN. It happens really often that criminals are creating a public free Hotspot to steal someone’s data.

    Free VPN vs Paid VPN

    • Free VPNs will collect information about user and may sell it to advertisers.
    • Paid VPNs will not log user’s activity on the internet and will keep it private.
